Broccoli Chicken Divan

Description

Chicken Divan with broccoli is a quick and easy casserole that's sure to be a hit with the family!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chopped fresh broccoli
  • 1 ½ cups cubed, cooked chicken meat
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of broccoli soup
  • ⅓ cup milk
  • ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons dried bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).

Step 2

Place broccoli in a saucepan with enough water to cover. Bring to a boil and cook until tender, about 5 minutes. Drain.

Step 3

Place the cooked broccoli in a 9 inch pie plate. Top with the chicken. In a bowl, mix the soup and milk, and pour over the chicken. Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese. Mix the melted butter with the bread crumbs, and sprinkle over the cheese.

Step 4

Bake in the preheated oven until casserole is bubbly and the top is golden brown, about 15 minutes. Serve and enjoy!
